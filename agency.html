AGENCY

Brilliance Gems are often summoned to travel internationally to represent rare collections and artefacts. In 2016 our Director and Chief designer, Marthese Agius was summoned to represent the World’s rarest and exclusive collection of Argyle Pink and Red diamond pieces to showcase throughout India, the Middle East and Malaysia, hosting private screenings with the Royals and elite of those regions.

An exclusive “Pink Carpet” event with the Who’s who of the region drew quite a lot of attention to this magnificent collection that featured a very rare Cartier offering.

We are proud and thrilled to represent our own creations and stunning complementary, historical jewellery, as well as your private collection.
