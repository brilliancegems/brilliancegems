HISTORY

Since the 1990s , director and lead designer, Marthese Agius has immersed herself in the international gem industry as an Opal specialist, with considerable experience in Diamonds, Pearls and other gems. She has worked with exquisite gems, mainly from Australia, but also with a vast collection of International gems.

Working with the likes of Valentino, Kenzo, Emmanuelle Ungaro, Sonia Rykiel and many leading international designers, Marthese was scouted as head co-ordinator for Dubai Fashion Week and Dubai Shopping Festival.

Her many years of experience in the Gem and Fashion worlds has given her an eye for detail and a sense of style that has served many international celebrities from Royalty through to music icons, working with some of the world’s greats.Our team has attended many international trade shows and events, representing Australia and it's Brilliant Natural Gems.

Our reputation is of the greatest importance to us as we strive to become an international benchmark for excellence.We ship across the globe as well as offer duty free pricing to our clients.
