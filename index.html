Brilliance, Gems of Australia International is a world leader in Natural Australian Gems, specialising in Australian Opal, Argyle Diamonds, International Pearls and gemstones of the World.

Our range of exclusive Black fire Opal, Crystal Opal and Boulder Opal are complemented by our acclaimed collection of rare pink diamonds and exclusive Pearls.

Our dedication and integrity to our passion has seen us recognised as one of the World’s most notable designers and manufacturers of world best gems.

SERVICE WITH INTEGRITY

At Brilliance, Gems of Australia International we believe in uncovering and presenting to you the unrivalled brilliance of what nature has to offer.

We take our time in designing the perfect piece to add to your collection, each designed to reflect your taste and meet your needs.

INTERNATIONAL RECOGNITION

Through our dedication to brilliance, we have developed an enviable international reputation, with recognition from many Governments and
Kingdoms that have designated Brilliance as preferred supplier and trusted consultants in the industry.

Brilliance has travelled the world to design, promote and exhibit rare collections that have never before been
viewed and we bring that same pursuit of excellence to the creation of your treasured collection.
