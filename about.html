ABOUT

Brilliance, Gems of Australia International specialises in Australian Opals, Diamonds and Pearls. We are agents for exclusive museum pieces and are unsurpassed in the acquisition of rare, timeless pieces as well as exquisite every day jewellery.

Our quality has guaranteed us many repeat orders from international clients. Our dedication to fulfilling your requests and meeting your desires to create your perfect piece, is what makes our team truly outstanding.

We represent the creation of brilliance, prestige, elegance - all with absolute integrity, and this melding of detail and inspiration shows in our rare and exclusive collection.

ROYALTY

Our lead designer Marthese Agius has designed and manufactured many exclusive pieces for Monarchs and International names from all over the globe.

We have been honoured to be chosen to design and manufacture such inspiring creations.

VIP APPOINTMENTS

Our lead designer has received many invitations internationally to host VIP viewings, working with clients from Royalty to Poliical figres and Celebrities, such a King Errisson, Neil Diamond, Deborah Harry, and numerous celebrities from the entertainment world.

AGENCY

Brilliance Gems are often summoned to travel internationally to represent rare collections and artefacts. In 2016 our Director and Chief designer, Marthese Agius was approached to represent the World’s rarest and exclusive collection of Argyle Pink and Red diamond pieces on a tour of India, the Middle East and Malaysia, hosting private screenings with the Royal’s and elite of those regions. An exclusive “Pink Carpet” event with the Who’s Who of the region drew quite a lot of attention to this magnificent collection that featured a very rare Cartier offering.

We are proud and thrilled to represent our own creations as well as stunning complementary, historical jewellery pieces.

Should you have a rare museum piece that you would like to find a buyer for or raise it's profile, we also arrange VIP screenings internationally. Please contact our Concierge.

OUR TEAM

Brilliance gems take pride in it's exceptionally talented team with each member specialising in either Opal, Diamonds, Pearls and general Gemmology. All staff are multilingual and able to assist with your every need.

GUARANTEE OF BRILLIANCE

All of our brilliant creations have a world-wide guarantee and while we pride ourselves on our attention to quality, any item if faulty will be replaced at no cost to the client. All who purchase an “Investment creation” will receive a Certificate of Authenticity and Valuation, including a digital image of the piece scanned onto the document, which is then signed by our licensed valuer. All Brilliance Gems pieces meet the high demands of our ethical jewellery policy.

GIVING BACK

We have been so privileged in what we do. Our gratitude to our clients for trusting us in an important aspect of their life enables us to help those who are not so privileged. We support many foundations, with a percentage of our proceeds going to charity. We also take pride in sponsoring many international events from medical to social.
